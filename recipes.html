Classic Beef Burger

Juicy, flavorful beef burgers with fresh vegetables and special sauce. Perfect for a weekend barbecue or family dinner.

450
Calories
25g
Protein
Medium
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 pickles, sliced
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties, about 1cm thick. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness, or until desired doneness.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. While patties are cooking, toast the burger buns cut-side down in the pan until golden.
  6. Mix mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard to create the special sauce.
  7. Spread the sauce on both halves of the buns.
  8. Assemble the burgers: bottom bun, lettuce, patty with cheese, tomato slices, onion, pickles, and top bun.
  9. Serve immediately while hot. Enjoy!

Creamy Pesto Pasta

Fresh basil pesto combined with cream creates a rich, flavorful sauce that coats every strand of pasta perfectly.

520
Calories
18g
Protein
Easy
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, make the pesto: In a food processor, combine basil, garlic, pine nuts, and parmesan cheese.
  3. Pulse until coarsely chopped, then slowly drizzle in olive oil while processing until smooth.
  4. Season pesto with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. In a large pan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until warm (do not boil).
  6. Stir in the prepared pesto until well combined.
  7. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  8. Add pasta to the pan with pesto cream sauce, toss to coat. Add pasta water if needed to thin the sauce.
  9. Garnish with cherry tomatoes and extra parmesan. Serve immediately.

Grilled Chicken Salad

Tender grilled chicken breast served over a bed of fresh mixed greens with balsamic vinaigrette dressing.

380
Calories
42g
Protein
Easy
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Let marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side, or until internal temperature reaches 165Â°F (74Â°C).
  4. Remove chicken from grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. While chicken rests, prepare the dressing: whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  7. Slice the grilled chicken and arrange on top of the salad.
  8. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and sprinkle with feta cheese.
  9. Serve immediately with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice if desired.

Beautiful Salmon Bowl

Fresh salmon with nutrient-rich greens, grains, and a tangy dressing. A perfect healthy meal that's both delicious and satisfying.

485
Calories
38g
Protein
Easy
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (150g each)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 2 cups mixed greens (kale, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup edamame beans
  • 1/4 cup red cabbage, shredded
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F (200Â°C). Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Place salmon on a baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until fish flakes easily.
  3. While salmon cooks, prepare the dressing: whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and grated ginger.
  4. Cook quinoa or brown rice according to package directions and let cool slightly.
  5. Steam edamame beans for 3-4 minutes until tender.
  6. Assemble the bowls: Start with a base of cooked grains, then add mixed greens.
  7. Top with flaked salmon, avocado slices, edamame, red cabbage, and julienned carrot.
  8. Drizzle with the soy-ginger dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy this nutritious, colorful meal!

Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with fresh mozzarella, basil, and simple tomato sauce. Simple ingredients that create an unforgettable flavor.

320
Calories
15g
Protein
Medium
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • 500g pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cornmeal for dusting (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475Â°F (245Â°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. On a floured surface, stretch or roll the pizza dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Transfer dough to a pizza pan or parchment paper dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Mix tomato sauce with minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Spread evenly over the dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
  5. Arrange mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and season with a pinch of salt.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  8. Remove from oven and immediately top with fresh basil leaves.
  9. Let cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!
